Astra Raksell

Astra Raksell is a millennia-old "Super Space Adventurer" forged from the universe's purest, uncontained magic. Roaming the cosmos with a shapeshifting plasma arm and an inexhaustible appetite for coffee and chocolate, they take on the galaxy's most dangerous jobs. Despite being a literal "glass cannon," this ever-curious figure is always chasing the next grand adventure—and ensuring they leave every planet a little better than they found it.

Appearance

Astra has a striking and highly distinct physical presence, characterized most notably by their vibrant purple hair worn in a messy, high ponytail with a prominent, sweeping orange underlayer. They possess striking heterochromia, featuring one bright blue eye and one deep yellow eye... Their most unmistakable physical trait is their right arm. From the shoulder down, the limb is entirely composed of luminous, uncontained purple Aurora energy that constantly emits a plasma-like smoke.

Civilian Form

In their standard, day-to-day attire, Astra leans into a practical, sporty, and tomboyish aesthetic. They wear a long, open black jacket featuring thick blue and white stripes on the sleeves and large, yellow-outlined stars decorating the front and back...

Battle Form

When entering combat and activating the Aurora Core, Astra undergoes a magical transformation into a heavily armored, tactical bodysuit...

Personality

Astra is a genuine, tomboyish ray of sunshine with a stubborn streak and wild gremlin energy. Confident but not loud, they are driven by an inexhaustible curiosity about the universe. They operate on logic and evidence, but when the mood strikes, they commit to their wild ideas 100%.

  • Highly friendly and exceptionally open when it comes to trust.
  • Incredibly calm and not hot-headed at all.
  • Demeanor leans heavily toward playful rather than serious, accompanied by a teasing sense of humor.

Biography

Background / Early Life

Astra wasn't born, but willed into existence from raw "Aurora" energy—the elemental combination of all ancient magical forces. They woke up in a sealed capsule with zero personal history. Their only memory is a vague, persistent dream of a platinum blue-haired woman they call "mother"...

Current

Astra started out as a bounty hunter, tackling messy situations and political drama across the galaxy. Eventually, the mercenary life gave way to their true calling: being a full-time "Super Space Adventurer."

Skills and Abilities

  • Raw Aurora Physiology: Made of unrefined, elementless magic. Technically stronger than anything in the universe.
  • Uncontained Right Arm: Operating outside normal physical rules, the arm can stretch, shapeshift, and absorb or release vast amounts of energy.
  • Gift Resonance: Gift users sense them as an ancient, overwhelming presence.
  • Supernatural Speed: Operating as a definitive glass cannon, Astra’s survival relies entirely on movement.
  • Indomitable Will: The absolute refusal to break, yield, or surrender, regardless of how insurmountable the odds might be.

Notable Items

  • Aurora Core: A belt buckle that triggers their combat transformation.
  • Claiomh Solais & Failnaught: A physical sword and blaster forged from the same source as their arm.
  • Mac an Luin: An energy blade materialized directly from their right arm.

Preferences

Likes: Chocolate, Coffee, Thermal vents, High-speed adventures, Writing, Drawing.

Dislikes: Large monsters (especially without pay), Democracies, Heavy weaponry, Insects, Bigotry.

Quotations

"No job is worth doing for less than the cost of a good meal and a coffee. Everything else is negotiable — emphasis on negotiable."
"Chocolate first, everything else second."

Trivia

  • The Uncanny Recognition: Humans across the galaxy vaguely recognize Astra as an "older," familiar face, despite no records existing.
  • Left-Handed: Astra relies on their left hand for everything.
  • Astra's Code: Astra operates by six strict rules including eating chocolate first, resting hard, and leaving every place better than they found it.
