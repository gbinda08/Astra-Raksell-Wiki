Astra Raksell

There is no species classification for Astra Raksell. Not really. The closest anyone has gotten is "Aurorian" — a term that exists more as a placeholder than an answer, a question mark dressed up as a category.

Appearance

Astra has a striking and highly distinct physical presence. Vibrant purple hair worn in a messy high ponytail with a sweeping orange underlayer. Striking heterochromia — one bright blue eye, one deep yellow. Their most unmistakable trait is their right arm: from the shoulder down, the limb is entirely composed of luminous, uncontained Aurora energy, constantly emitting plasma-like purple smoke that drifts lazily and inevitably, the visual confirmation that this arm was never going to pass for normal.

Civilian Form

Day-to-day Astra leans into a practical, sporty, tomboyish aesthetic. A long open black jacket with thick blue and white stripes on the sleeves, large yellow-outlined stars on the front and back. Goggles pushed up — always ready to go, always just came from something. The orange hair streak loose, the ponytail functional. Someone who cares enough to have a look and not enough to overthink it.

Battle Form

Pressing the Aurora Core belt buckle changes everything. Armor materializes, the right arm is stripped bare, and the read shifts completely. Civilian Astra says hey, want to grab coffee? Battle Astra says I've done this a thousand times. The star mask obscures the eyes. The silhouette tightens. This is not someone who needs to worry about defense — which, statistically speaking, is a problem they have anyway.

Personality

A ray of sunshine. Genuinely, consistently, in a way that has nothing to do with performance. Kind to strangers across species lines. Curious about everything. Stubborn in the specific way of someone who has decided something and will not be moved by logic alone — only evidence, and sometimes not even then. Confident without being loud about it. Tomboyish ease that just fits. Wild when the mood strikes, which it does, with full commitment.

  • Extremely friendly and wide open when it comes to trust.
  • Completely calm — not a hot-headed bone in their body.
  • Playful by default, teasing sense of humor, serious when it actually counts.
  • Left hand on the coffee, left hand on the sword hilt, left hand already reaching for the chocolate.

Biography

Origin

Astra wasn't born. They were willed into existence — the last act of something that had just enough left in it to try one final time. Made entirely of raw Aurora energy, the elemental combination of every magical force that ever existed in the old world. They woke up in a sealed capsule with zero personal history, no before, just the moment of waking and a vague persistent dream: a platinum-blue-haired woman looking at them like she knew exactly what she was doing. Astra calls her "mother." It's not quite the right word, but it's the closest one available.

Whoever sealed that capsule left no instructions, no map, no list of objectives. Just the world — and underneath Astra's ribcage, something that feels like unfinished business with no name attached to it.

Career

Bounty hunting was the starting point. Moving through space, taking contracts, dealing with whatever needed dealing with. Galactic federations with more political drama than anyone should have to sit through, and Astra sitting through it anyway because they genuinely find it more entertaining than frustrating. At some point the bounty hunting stopped being an identity and became an excuse to keep moving.

"Super Space Adventurer" isn't an official title. It's just the most honest description of what Astra does now — go places, get involved, see what's out there. The thirst for adventure is not a phase they're going to grow out of. Millennia in and still genuinely excited about what's around the next corner.

Skills & Abilities

  • Raw Aurora Physiology — Made of unrefined, elementless magic. Technically stronger than any shaped power in the universe, still somehow running on vibes and momentum.
  • Uncontained Right Arm — Stretches infinitely, shapeshifts, absorbs and releases vast amounts of energy. Operates outside normal physical rules and never lets you forget it.
  • Gift Resonance — Gift users sense them as something ancient, vast, and improperly localized. Earth-born Gift users feel it the most specifically — like a word recognizing the language it belongs to.
  • Supernatural Speed — Speed is how Astra survives. The entire combat strategy is built around this.
  • Indomitable Will — Absolute refusal to break, yield, or stop regardless of the odds. Statistically inexplicable survival rate. The universe has opinions about this. Astra doesn't.

Weapons

  • Aurora Core — Belt buckle. Triggers combat transformation, deploys armor, unleashes full arm capability.
  • Claiomh Solais — Physical sword forged from the same source as the arm.
  • Failnaught — Blaster, same origin.
  • Mac an Luin — Energy blade materialized directly from the right arm.

Operator's Code

Six rules. Non-negotiable. Written down because some things need to be written down.

"No job is worth doing for less than the cost of a good meal and a coffee. Everything else is negotiable — emphasis on negotiable."
"Large monsters are personal. Charge accordingly, or don't take it at all."
"Chocolate first, everything else second."
"Rest as hard as you move. A body running on empty isn't brave, it's just broken."
"Find the thermal somewhere. Every planet's got one if you look hard enough."
"Leave every place better than you found it. Or at least try. Explosions happen."

Preferences

Likes: Chocolate, Coffee, Thermal vents, High-speed adventures, Writing, Drawing, Sketching.

Dislikes: Large monsters (especially without pay), Democracies, Heavy weaponry, Insects, Rudeness, Bigotry.

Trivia

  • The Uncanny Recognition — Humans across the galaxy look at Astra like they recognize them. No records exist that predate their waking. Nobody can explain it. Astra attributes it to personal charm and possibly the arm.
  • Left-handed — Everything. Coffee, sword, chocolate, all of it.
  • Glass cannon — Speed 100, Magic 95, Defense 30, Luck 10. The math is terrifying and yet here they are.
  • The arm does not come off — It has been asked.
